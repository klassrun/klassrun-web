We specialise in automating the most time-consuming parts of school administration — so teachers can focus on teaching, and school owners can focus on growing.

Our products are built around the Nigerian curriculum, WAEC and NECO standards, and real infrastructure challenges Nigerian schools face — low connectivity, manual processes, and limited IT support.
