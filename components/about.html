About us

Klassrun Technologies Ltd is a Lagos-based software company building AI-powered school management systems, web applications, and mobile apps for Nigerian schools and organisations across all sectors. We specialise in turning complex, manual processes into fast, intelligent software.

Our EdTech products are built specifically around the Nigerian curriculum, WAEC and NECO standards, and the real infrastructure challenges schools face — including low connectivity, manual record-keeping, and limited IT support. Beyond education, we build custom digital solutions for businesses that need to move faster.
